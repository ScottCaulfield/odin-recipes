Chicken Wrap

Description

A delicious and easy to make chicken wrap

  • Southern fried chicken
  • Tortilla wrap
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Hot sauce
  1. Cook the southern fried chicken in whichever way
  2. Lay the wrap flat, spread some mayonnaise on it, and add a few dashes of hot sauce
  3. Shread and put the lettuce on the wrap
  4. Place the chicken on top
  5. Wrap up tightly
