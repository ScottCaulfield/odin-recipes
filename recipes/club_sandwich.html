Club Sandwich

Description

It's just really good

  • White bread
  • Chicken
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  1. Cook the chicken and the bacon
  2. Lightly toast the bread and spread with mayo
  3. Layer lettuce on the bottom slice, and put the bacon on top
  4. Place the middle slice
  5. Layer tomatoes on that slice, then put the chicken on top
  6. Place the final slice of bread
